  - Extortion/blackmail: additional penalties

- **Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA)** - 17 U.S.C. § 1201
  - Anti-circumvention provisions may apply to some uses

- **National Computer Security Center (NCSC)** Guidelines
  - Various agency-specific regulations

### European Union
- **GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)**
  - Consent requirements for data processing
  - Right to be forgotten
  - Data breach notifications
  - Fines up to €20 million or 4% of turnover

- **Computer Misuse Act (UK)** - Computer Misuse Act 1990
  - Unauthorized access: up to 2 years imprisonment
  - Unauthorized modification: up to 5 years imprisonment
  - With intent to commit/facilitate crime: up to 10 years

- **French Penal Code** - Articles 323-1 to 323-7
- **German StGB (Strafgesetzbuch)** - § 202a, § 263a

### Other Jurisdictions
- Similar computer fraud/access laws in most countries
- Telecommunications regulations (India ITA, 2000)
- Privacy laws (Australia Privacy Act, Canada PIPEDA)
